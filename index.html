About Me

My name is Marta, I'm 25. I graduated Landscape Architecture and English Philology. I'm a self-learning passionist of creating websites.

Studying landscape architecture taught me to plan gardens and green spaces that are pleasing to the eye and at the same time functional. When creating websites, you do the same - you combine both tech and design skills to make functional and aesthetic websites. Those skills helped me a lot when learning programming.

To polish my coding skills I watch many youtube tutorials ( especially from freeCodeCamp), take courses on Udemy and most important - practice by creating my own projects. Except creating websites, I'm interested in manual and automation testing and artificial intelligence. Testing my own projects as well as finding bugs in popular websites gives me a kick.
